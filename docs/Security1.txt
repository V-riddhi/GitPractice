2. Black Basta Ransomware

How it Happens:

Black Basta is malware that locks your files and asks for money to unlock them. It spreads through phishing emails or fake software updates.

How to Fix It:

Disconnect from the network.

Do not pay the ransom.

Restore from a backup.


Prevention:

Backup files regularly.

Avoid clicking suspicious email links.

Keep your software updated.


Real-World Example:

In 2022, Deutsche Windtechnik, a German company, was hit by Black Basta, causing system shutdowns.
